# Pricing & Engagement Models

Fortellar offers flexible pricing models designed to align with organizational needs, project scope, and complexity.

## Overview
Pricing depends on the type of engagement, level of customization, and required expertise. Fortellar works with clients to define clear scope and deliver measurable outcomes.

## Engagement Models

### Fixed-Scope Projects
- Ideal for well-defined initiatives
- Examples: assessments, audits, short-term implementations
- Predictable cost and timeline

### Time & Materials
- Flexible for evolving projects
- Suitable for ongoing development and support
- Billed based on actual effort

### Managed Services
- Continuous support and optimization
- Includes monitoring, maintenance, and improvements
- Monthly subscription model

## Typical Engagement Areas
- CloudOps implementation
- Security and compliance programs
- Disaster recovery planning
- AI readiness and strategy

## Value Proposition
- Transparent pricing
- Scalable engagement models
- Focus on measurable business outcomes

For detailed pricing, Fortellar recommends contacting a representative for a tailored proposal.
