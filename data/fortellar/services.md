# Fortellar Services

Fortellar provides a range of technology services designed to support modern enterprise environments.

## CloudOps
Fortellar helps organizations design, deploy, and manage cloud infrastructure. This includes:
- AWS cloud architecture
- Infrastructure as Code (Terraform)
- CI/CD pipeline implementation
- Cost optimization and FinOps
- Observability and monitoring

## Security & Compliance
Fortellar builds secure cloud environments and ensures compliance with industry standards:
- Security architecture design
- IAM and access control
- Encryption and data protection
- SOC 2 readiness and compliance support
- Continuous monitoring and threat detection

## Disaster Recovery & Resilience
Fortellar helps organizations prepare for and recover from failures:
- Backup and recovery strategies
- High availability design
- Incident response planning
- Business continuity solutions

## AI Readiness
Fortellar enables organizations to adopt AI safely and effectively:
- AI readiness assessments
- Data and infrastructure evaluation
- Governance and compliance for AI systems
- Integration of AI into existing workflows

Fortellar’s services are designed to be scalable, secure, and aligned with business goals.
