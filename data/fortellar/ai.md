# AI Readiness and Adoption

Fortellar helps organizations prepare for and adopt artificial intelligence in a secure and scalable way.

## AI Readiness Assessment
Fortellar evaluates an organization’s current capabilities, including:
- Data availability and quality
- Infrastructure readiness
- Security and governance
- Business use cases

## Implementation Strategy
- Identifying high-value AI opportunities
- Designing scalable AI architectures
- Integrating AI into existing systems

## Governance and Compliance
- Responsible AI practices
- Data privacy considerations
- Risk management frameworks

## Operational Integration
- Embedding AI into workflows
- Monitoring AI system performance
- Ensuring reliability and scalability

Fortellar focuses on practical AI adoption, helping organizations move from exploration to real-world impact.
