# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## What does Fortellar do?
Fortellar is a technology services company that helps organizations design, build, and operate modern cloud environments with a focus on CloudOps, security, disaster recovery, and AI readiness.

## What industries does Fortellar support?
Fortellar supports a wide range of industries, including healthcare, finance, and enterprise technology.

## Does Fortellar help with cloud migration?
Yes, Fortellar helps organizations migrate workloads from on-premises environments to the cloud and modernize existing systems.

## How does Fortellar ensure security and compliance?
Fortellar embeds security controls into infrastructure using automation, including encryption, IAM policies, monitoring, and compliance frameworks such as HIPAA.

## What is AI readiness?
AI readiness refers to an organization’s ability to successfully adopt artificial intelligence, including having the right data, infrastructure, governance, and processes in place.

## Does Fortellar provide ongoing support?
Yes, Fortellar offers managed services to continuously monitor, optimize, and improve cloud environments.

## How can I get started?
You can start by contacting Fortellar for an assessment or consultation to evaluate your current environment and identify opportunities for improvement.
