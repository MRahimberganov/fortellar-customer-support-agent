# Disaster Recovery & Resilience

Fortellar helps organizations build resilient systems that can recover quickly from disruptions, ensuring business continuity and minimal downtime.

## Overview
Unexpected outages, cyber incidents, and infrastructure failures can significantly impact business operations. Fortellar designs disaster recovery strategies that align with business requirements and risk tolerance.

## Capabilities
- Disaster recovery planning and architecture
- Backup and restore strategies
- Cross-region and multi-zone replication
- Recovery Time Objective (RTO) and Recovery Point Objective (RPO) planning
- Automated failover and failback solutions
- Continuous resilience testing

## Benefits
- Minimized downtime during incidents
- Protection against data loss
- Faster recovery of critical systems
- Increased customer trust and reliability
- Alignment with regulatory requirements

## Use Cases
- Protecting critical healthcare or financial systems
- Ensuring uptime for customer-facing applications
- Building resilient cloud-native architectures
