# HIPAA Compliance & Security

Fortellar helps healthcare organizations and partners achieve and maintain HIPAA compliance through secure cloud architecture, automation, and continuous monitoring.

## Overview
The HIPAA Security Rule requires organizations to protect sensitive health information (ePHI) through administrative, physical, and technical safeguards. Fortellar embeds these controls directly into cloud environments.

## Capabilities
- Secure cloud architecture design
- Encryption of data at rest and in transit
- Identity and Access Management (IAM) policies
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
- Audit logging and monitoring
- Automated compliance checks and reporting

## Key Compliance Areas
- Access control and identity management
- Data encryption and protection
- Logging and auditing
- Incident response and breach detection
- Disaster recovery and data availability

## Benefits
- Reduced risk of data breaches
- Continuous compliance monitoring
- Faster audit readiness
- Improved security posture
- Confidence in handling sensitive healthcare data

## 2024 Updates
Recent updates to HIPAA emphasize stricter security requirements, including mandatory MFA, stronger encryption standards, and faster recovery expectations.
